From Scatter to Line

I investigate the regression line a bit deeper and found a more intuitive way of describing the evolution of a line from scattered dots. Usually a regression line is calculated with minimizing errors. But the natural approach of working out a simple regression line from data points is to hang a standard deviation line on the point of averages ($\bar{x}$, $\bar{y}$) and adjust it with the correlation coefficient.
